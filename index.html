About the Project

A totally necessary martini database, with a twist

Listen, we are living in a golden age of martinis. Espresso martinis are fueling the masses, the East River water is making dirty martinis dirtier than ever, and some bartenders are doing things with gin that should probably require a permit.

Enter The Martini Project your spirited guide to NYC’s best dirty martinis. There’s no crying over spilt milk, but we allow for the occasional tear over a tasteless martini.

This is not just a list. This is a mission. And it comes with blue cheese stuffed olives.
